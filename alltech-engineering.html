ALLTECH ENGINEERING

At Alltech, our engineering team uses state of the Art design and engineering tools such as 3D scanning for reverse engineering.

Software capabilities:


  • - Master Cam X-9 

  • - AutoCad Inventor 2016 (can read & write Pro-E, Catia)

Process for development:

  • - Identifying the component to be re-engineered or transitioned to the most suitable process.

  • - Collaborating with our customers to assess the requirements of how the component is to ultimately be utilized.

  • - Eventually we develop a 3D drawing; commission the tooling and casting dies to be built and begin the Production Part Approval Process (PPAP).

  • - After approval of First Article Sample, and we have established Quality Assurance procedures for production, we begin mass-production of the component and arrange logistics and final delivery according to client’s requirements.
